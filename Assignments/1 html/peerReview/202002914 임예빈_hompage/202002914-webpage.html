ABOUT

CINEMA TALK is one of the most popular audio magazines among film lovers since 2020. The audio magazine is delivered by email once a month along with audio files,  photos and illustrations. In addition, paper magazines are delivered to your home. We introduce various movies and wonderful stories related to those films.  There is an interview corner with film directors, writers, actors, experts and those who have connection with the film.  We talk about not only movies, but also culture, fashion, and life-everything you can learn through films. We invite you to a wider perspective toward the world.
